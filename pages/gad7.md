<h1>
    Generalized Anxiety  Disorder (GAD-7) Scale
</h1>
<p>
    The Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD)  questionnaire (Spitzer et al., 2006)
    is a 7-item scale designed to measure  generalised anxiety disorder.
    Different versions of the scale have been created  which include a subset of
    the original 7 items.
</p>
<p>
    <a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.1001/archinte.166.10.1092">
        Spitzer, R. L., Kroenke,  K., Williams, J. B., &amp; Löwe, B. (2006). A
        brief measure for assessing  generalized anxiety disorder: the GAD-7.
        Archives of internal  medicine, 166(10), 1092-1097.
        http://dx.doi.org/10.1001/archinte.166.10.1092
    </a>
</p>
